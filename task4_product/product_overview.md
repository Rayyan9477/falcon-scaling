# Co-Investment Intelligence Accelerator

## The Problem

Fund managers, GPs, and capital raisers waste months trying to figure out which family offices actually co-invest, who they co-invest with, and how to get into co-investment deal flow.

The co-investment market among family offices is booming — **69% of family offices co-invest or plan to** (UBS Global Family Office Report 2024). But the "who invests with whom" network is opaque, scattered across regulatory filings, press releases, and industry rumors.

Current options are either:
- **Too expensive**: PitchBook ($20K+/yr), Fintrx ($10K+/yr) — out of reach for emerging managers
- **Too generic**: LinkedIn research, conference networking — unstructured, time-consuming
- **Non-existent**: No product specifically maps FO co-investment networks at an accessible price point

- **Emerging fund managers** ($50M-$500M AUM) raising capital from family offices
- **PE/VC associates** responsible for LP targeting and capital introduction

- **Placement agents** working fundraising mandates
- **Independent sponsors** seeking co-investment partners for deals

## The Product ($197 One-Time)

A three-component bundle delivering immediate, actionable co-investment intelligence:

### Component 1: Excel Dashboard — "FO Co-Investment Scorer & Mapper"
- 200 family offices scored for co-investment propensity (1-10 scale)
- Accessibility scoring for outreach readiness (Green/Yellow/Red)
- Known co-investor relationships and fund relationships included per FO
- Filterable by region, sector, check size, co-invest frequency
- Pre-built pivot views by region and sector

### Component 2: Written Guide — "The Family Office Approach Playbook"
- How family offices make co-investment decisions
- The 5 types of FO co-investors and how to approach each

### Component 3: Custom GPT — "FO Co-Investment Advisor"
- Interactive AI advisor trained on co-investment methodology
- Ask: "I'm raising a $75M climate tech fund — which FOs should I target?"
- Returns personalized targeting lists with reasoning and approach strategy
